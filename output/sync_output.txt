========== 读者-写者问题 ==========
Reader 1 读取 sharedData=0
Reader 2 读取 sharedData=0
Reader 3 读取 sharedData=0
Writer 2 写入 sharedData=1
Writer 1 写入 sharedData=2
Reader 3 读取 sharedData=2
Reader 2 读取 sharedData=2
Reader 1 读取 sharedData=2
Writer 2 写入 sharedData=3
Writer 1 写入 sharedData=4
Reader 3 读取 sharedData=4
Reader 2 读取 sharedData=4
Reader 1 读取 sharedData=4
结果: 多个读者可并发读；写者独占访问；等待写者优先，减少写者饥饿。
